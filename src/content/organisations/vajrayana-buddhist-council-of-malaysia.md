The Vajrayana Buddhist Council of Malaysia (VBCM) represents the Vajrayana (Tibetan) Buddhist community in Malaysia. As the national body for Vajrayana organisations, VBCM coordinates activities, supports Vajrayana teachers and centres, and promotes understanding of Vajrayana Buddhist traditions.

VBCM brings together various Vajrayana Buddhist centres and groups from different lineages, including Gelug, Kagyu, Nyingma, and Sakya, creating a unified voice for the Vajrayana community in Malaysia.

## Unity in Diversity

VBCM embraces the rich diversity of Vajrayana Buddhist lineages while fostering cooperation and mutual support among different Vajrayana communities in Malaysia.

## Key Activities

- Coordinating among Vajrayana Buddhist centres and groups
- Supporting visiting Vajrayana teachers and rinpoches
- Organising Vajrayana Buddhist ceremonies and empowerments
- Promoting inter-lineage dialogue and understanding
